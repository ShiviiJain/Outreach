A Conversation with Dr. Vivek Lall at UC Berkeley

Speaker Series India @ Berkeley, in collaboration with Formula Electric @ Berkeley, is excited to welcome the esteemed Dr. Vivek Lall this upcoming Tuesday.

With a solid foundation in mechanical engineering during his undergraduate studies and advanced expertise in aeronautical engineering at the master's level, Dr. Lall has become a prominent figure within the aerospace and defense sectors. His significant contributions to industry giants like Lockheed Martin, Boeing, and currently General Atomics have been widely recognized. We look forward to having a stimulating discussion with him and hope you can join us in the journey.

Join us in gaining key insights about this ever-evolving global landscape, and learn more about what the future has in store for the aerospace and defense industries.
